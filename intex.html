Welcome to Maja's MTB journey


Maja started her MTB journey in the fall of 2020. During 2022 her interest and love for the sport reached new heights and meeting two of the best women in the sport during SM just added to this. On this page you will be able to follow her, check out stats from former competitions and see upcoming events.

About Maja


Maja was born in March 2013 and since then she's been in constant motion. She loves sports and when she's not on her bike she also competes in both swimming and tennis. MTB is her passion and even if 2022 was her first year of competeing in MTB she came in 3rd place in SWECUP. So 2023 we are looking forward to follow her progress on a brand new custombuilt bike.
